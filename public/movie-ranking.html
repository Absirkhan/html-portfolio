Top 3 movies of all time.

Nightcrawler

The film explores the dark obsession and the dangerous consequences that unfold when ambition and ethics collide in the pursuit of success.

Ford vs Ferrari

"Ford v Ferrari" is an exhilarating true story that chronicles the high-stakes battle between automotive giants Ford and Ferrari during the 1960s. With skilled driver Ken Miles and visionary engineer Carroll Shelby at the helm, the film showcases their relentless pursuit to build a revolutionary race car and challenge Ferrari's dominance at the prestigious 24 Hours of Le Mans race.

Rush

"Rush" is a gripping biographical drama that depicts the intense rivalry between Formula 1 drivers James Hunt and Niki Lauda during the 1970s. The film explores their contrasting personalities, the dangerous world of professional racing, and the ultimate pursuit of glory on and off the track.
